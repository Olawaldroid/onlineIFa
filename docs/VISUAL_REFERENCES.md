# Ifá visual reference register

The site’s vector system is based on identifiable ritual objects, not a generic
“African pattern” library. The SVGs are original, simplified drawings; they do
not trace or embed the reference photographs.

## Motion rule

The consultation animation follows the documented gesture rather than shaking
in place: the midpoint leads away from the diviner, the free ends trail and
open outward, the eight plates turn, and the two arms settle together. The
implementation remains an educational approximation pending review against
practitioner-approved reference footage.

## Design guardrails

- Do not add an ornamental motif merely because it reads as “African.”
- Keep the tray’s central field quiet; carving belongs mainly to the rim.
- Preserve hand-worked asymmetry and material variation.
- Use documentary photographs with exact creator, collection, object number,
  and licence wherever those details are available.
- Keep vectors explanatory and restrained; one strong object is better than a
  field of unrelated symbols.
